About Kudremukha


Kudremukh is a mountain range and name of peak located in Chikkamagaluru district in Karnataka,India.It is also the name of a small hill station cum mining town situated near the mountain about 20 km from kalasa as well as beauty Known for its bio-diversity and mineral wealth, Kudremukh is located in the Chikmagalur district of Karnataka. In Kannada, Kudremukh means 'horse's face' and the place gets its name from a peak that resembles the face of a horse. The beautiful landscapes of Kurdremukh are a big crowd puller.
